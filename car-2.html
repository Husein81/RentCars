MG ZS

$ 17,400

MG ZS 2019 The ZS is a front wheel drive 4 door with 5 seats, powered by a 1.0L TURBO 3 engine that has 82 kW of power (at 5200 rpm) and 160 Nm of torque (at 1800 rpm) via a Six-speed Automatic. MG claims the ZS ESSENCE uses 6.7L/100km of Unleaded Petrol in the combined city and highway cycle while putting out 157g of CO2. It has a 48L fuel tank, meaning it should be able to travel 716km per full tank.
