Jeep Grand Cherokee SRT 2018

$ 31,770

Special trim packages include the Altitude, Upland, High Altitude, and Sterling Edition, which celebrates the Grand Cherokee's 25th anniversary. All but the SRT and Trackhawk feature a standard 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine that makes 295 horsepower and 260 pound-feet of torque. The flexible-fuel engine features variable-valve timing and lift as well as auto-stop/start technology and is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. Limited, Overland and Summit models offer an optional 3.0L EcoDiesel V6 with 240 horsepower and 420 lb-feet of torque
